Computer

A computer is an electronic device that processes data and performs various tasks based on a set of instructions, known as programs or software. Computers have become a vital part of modern life, used in homes, schools, businesses, and industries around the world. They come in different forms, including desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. At the core of every computer is a central processing unit (CPU), which acts as the brain, executing commands and managing operations. Computers are used for a wide range of activities.
